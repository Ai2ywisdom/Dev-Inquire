OUR GOAL

Empowering Zambia's Future: Our goal at Dev Inquire is to revolutionize mindsets across Zambia and beyond, fostering a culture of technological prowess and innovation. We aim to create an inclusive environment that nurtures, shares, and educates individuals of all ages and backgrounds in the realm of technology. Through a multifaceted approach encompassing school campaigns, workshops, and diverse programs, we strive to introduce and advance knowledge in fields such as machine learning, embedded systems, game development, and various programming disciplines. Our mission is to ignite curiosity, inspire learning, and equip every enthusiast with the tools and skills needed to thrive in the dynamic landscape of technology, paving the way for a brighter, more empowered tomorrow
